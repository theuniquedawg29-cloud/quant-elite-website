About Quant-Elite

Quant-Elite is a revolutionary AI-powered math learning platform designed to transform how students learn mathematics. Combining advanced quiz mechanics with intelligent AI mentorship, Quant-Elite provides personalized guidance tailored to each student's learning style and pace.
